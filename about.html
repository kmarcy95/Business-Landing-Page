About

From reading the numbers to building the tools.

An FP&A and strategic finance leader who doesn't stop at the analysis — I build the apps, AI workflows, and Excel tooling that turn it into action.

📊 FP&A & Strategic Finance ⚙ Builder / Full-Stack

I'm a finance and FP&A leader — full P&L ownership, forecasting, standard costing, and executive reporting across manufacturing, retail, distribution, and asset management. My job has always been to turn messy operational data into decisions senior leaders can act on, at companies like C.H. Guenther & Son, Lancer Worldwide, H-E-B, and AllianceBernstein.

What sets me apart is what happens after the analysis. Most teams paper over ERP and reporting gaps with spreadsheets, ad-hoc scripts, and tribal knowledge that walks out the door when someone leaves. I close those gaps for good — building the AI-enabled reporting, Blazor apps, Excel add-ins, and agentic workflows the team actually needs.

"Finance judgment plus the ability to build the fix myself is a rare pairing — and it's where I do my best work."

Based in San Antonio. Currently Financial Analysis Manager at C.H. Guenther & Son — open to senior finance-leadership roles, and taking on select consulting through KM Consulting. If you're hiring, the résumé is a click away; if your team needs the tooling, let's talk.
